Definition List

'h'
In HTML, the "h" followed by a number (from 1 to 6) represents a heading element. These are used to define headings in a webpage.
'p'
In HTML, the "p" element represents a paragraph. It is used to define and encapsulate a block of text that forms a distinct section or unit of content, such as a block of text in an article, a description, or any other standalone section of text.
